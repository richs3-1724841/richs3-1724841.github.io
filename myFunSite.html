Notable Characters

The most important ones

Spongebob Squarepants

Main Character, Lives in Pineapple

Spongebob is the focal point of the show. He flips burgers at the Krusty Krab, enjoys Jellyfishing and playing with his friend Patrick.

Patrick Star

Not the brains of the show, lives under a rock

Patrick's not the brightest specimen. He makes up for it with his sassy comedy, however. He's always hanging out with Spongebob, or eating something.

Squidward Tentacles

Always mad about something

Squidward is a co-worker and neighbor of Spongebob. He hates everything except playing clarinet.

Eugene Krabs

Entrepreneur, Businessman, Likes money

The owner of the Krusty Krab. Mr. Krabs is the money-loving crab that somehow has a whale as a daughter.
